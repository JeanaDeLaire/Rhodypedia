A Wicked Awesome App

Rhode Island might be the smallest state, but it has a whole lot of character and a gorgeous landscape riddled with hidden gems. From secret beaches to historical landmarks tucked away in recreational areas, the beauty of the nation's smallest state is truly inspiring. We created this app to offer all Rhode Islanders the chance to share their Rhody secrets with the world and showcase Rhody wonder.
